Security Policy

Account Security

  • Users must create strong passwords (minimum 12 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols).
  • Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text.
  • Users should never share their login credentials with others.

Data Protection

  • All sensitive user data (personal information, payment details) is encrypted in transit using HTTPS.
  • Only authorized personnel have access to sensitive data.

Session Management

  • User sessions automatically expire after 15 minutes of inactivity.
  • Users should log out after using the website, especially on public or shared devices.

Fraud Prevention

  • The system monitors for suspicious activity (e.g., multiple failed login attempts).
  • Accounts may be temporarily locked after repeated failed login attempts.

User Responsibilities

  • Users must provide accurate information and keep their account details up to date.
  • Any suspected security breach should be reported immediately to the support team.

Privacy

  • User data is never sold or shared with third parties except as required by law.
  • Users can request deletion of their account and personal data at any time.

Updates

  • This policy may be updated periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes.

This Security Policy describes how we protect your personal information when you visit www.jo24-paris.fr/billetterie (the "Site") or make a purchase there.

Personal Information Collected

When you visit the Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including your web browser, IP address, time zone, and some cookies installed on your device. We also collect information about the individual web pages or products you view, the websites or search terms that referred you to the Site, and information about how you interact with the Site. We refer to this automatically-collected information as "Device Information."

We collect Device Information using the following technologies:

  • Cookies:
    • _session_id: unique session token, allows our site to store information about your session (referrer, landing page, etc.).
    • _site_visit: no data held, persists for 30 minutes from the last visit. Used by our website host's internal statistics tracking system to record the number of visits.
    • _visits_uniq: no data held, expires midnight (relative to the visitor's location) of the following day. Calculates the number of visits to a page on the site by a unique customer.
    • _cart: unique identifier, persistent for 2 weeks, stores information related to your shopping cart.
    • _secure_session_id: unique session identifier.
    • storefront_digest: unique identifier, indefinite. If the store has a password, this is used to determine if the current visitor has access.
  • Log files: Track activity on the Site and collect data such as your IP address, browser type, Internet service provider, referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps.
  • Web beacons, tags, and pixels: Electronic files that record information about how you browse the Site.

Additionally, when you make or attempt to make a purchase through the Site, we collect certain information from you, including your name, shipping address, payment information (including credit card numbers), email address, and phone number. This is referred to as "Order Information."

When we use the term "Personal Information" in this Privacy Policy, we are referring to both Device Information and Order Information.

How Do We Use Your Personal Information?

  • To fulfill any orders placed through the Site (including to process your payment information and provide you with invoices and/or order confirmations).
  • To communicate with you.
  • To screen for fraud or risk.
  • To provide you with information or advertising relating to our products or services, in line with your preferences.
  • To improve and optimize our Site (for example, by generating analytics about how our customers browse and interact with the Site, and to assess the success of our advertising and marketing campaigns).
